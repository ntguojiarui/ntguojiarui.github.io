<h2 style="margin-top: 1em; padding-bottom: 0.1em; border-bottom: 1px solid var(--global-border-color); font-weight: bold;">Biography</h2>

I am a Ph.D. student in School of Computer Science at Peking University, advised by [Prof. Xiaolin Wang](https://cs.pku.edu.cn/info/1230/2031.htm) and [Prof. Tong Yang](https://yangtonghome.github.io/).
My research focuses on the design of high-performance data structures and algorithms, with a dedicated effort to bridge algorithmic theory and system performance in LLM inference acceleration, network telemetry, and database kernels.

I am currently a Research Intern at Alibaba Group, where I focus on enhancing the serving efficiency and deployment of large-scale models. 
Prior to my Ph.D. studies, I received my bachelor degree in Computer Science and Mathematics from Peking University in 2023.
